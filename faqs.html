Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I lose an item?

Report it immediately using the "Report Lost" page. Provide as much detail as possible to help us locate it.

How do I report a found item?

Use the "Report Found" page. You can include a photo if available. We'll add it to the lost & found list.

How can I claim an item?

Check the home page for available items and use the "Claim Item" page to submit your claim. It will be reviewed by an admin.

What if my claim is approved?

You'll be contacted via the information you provided. Pick up your item from the designated location.

How long are items kept?

Items are typically held for 30 days. After that, they may be donated or disposed of.

Who do I contact for help?

For urgent issues, contact the school office or use the admin panel if you're authorized.
